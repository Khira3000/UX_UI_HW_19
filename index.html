Khira Henry

Freelance UX/UI Designer

Hello, my name’s Khira, I’m a freelance UX/UI designer who has a keen interest in learning about people and translating this into great designs

My skills

My skills include Figma, user-centred research and design, and performing qualitative research.

User research

I approach user research as a crucial foundation for informed design decisions. My process involves a combination of qualitative and quantitative methods to gain a deep understanding of user needs, behaviors, and pain points.

User empathy

My approach to user empathy involves immersing myself in the user's perspective to understand their emotions, needs, and motivations. By adopting a human-centered design mindset, I aim to create experiences that resonate with users on a personal level.

Qualitative interviews

My approach to qualitative interviews involves creating a comfortable and open environment for participants, enabling them to share rich insights and perspectives. I employ a conversational style, combining structured and unstructured questions to delve into the user's experiences

Tools I use:

The tools I use include Figma, Visual Studio Code and Github.

Case Studies

Moving up

Younger generations have progressively become less familiar with the necessities relating to maintaining a home. Moving up is a one stop solution within the user’s pocket to help prepare them to become independent and take on the next chapter of their lives.

Young at Heart

Young at Heart, a charity dedicated to assisting families with children facing heart conditions. This organization organizes events and provides resources to support families during challenging times. Recognizing the passion behind this cause, our goal is to enhance their website by aligning it more closely with contemporary user interface conventions, aiming to optimize its usability and effectiveness.
